Crum Cakes Bakery

Made with C.A.R.E

At our bakery, we pride ourselves on creating delicious baked goods that are a symphony of flavors and textures. From heavenly cakes and decadent brownies to artisanal cupcakes and hand-dipped chocolate strawberries, our treats are crafted with passion and precision.

What started as a small, passion project to raise money for school soon turned into a dream come true: our own bakery. We are proud to serve the finest baked goods in Omaha and we continue to strive to provide the best cakes, cookies and all things delicious to our customers.

Cakes for Every Occasion

Imagine celebrating birthdays, weddings, graduations, or any occasion with a cake that's not just delicious but uniquely designed to reflect your style and sentiments. We thrive on personalization, turning your ideas into edible masterpieces.

Every cake we create is a blend of our passion for baking and your individual preferences, ensuring that your celebrations are sweet, memorable, and uniquely yours.
